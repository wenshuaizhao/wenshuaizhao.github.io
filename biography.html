Biography

Short Bio

    Wenshuai Zhao is currently a master student in the Department of Electronic Engineering, School of Information Science and Technology, Fudan University. Before that, he worked as a mechnical engineer in the during July 2014 to May 2017. He got his B.E. degree, with the major of mechanical design, manufacture and automation in the school of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering, Central South University, and graduated as Excellent Graduate of Hunan Province since June 2014.
